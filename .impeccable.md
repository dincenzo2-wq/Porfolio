## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary**: Brand managers, creative directors, and high-end couples looking for a video editor who doesn't just "cut" but "tells a story."
- **Context**: Browsing portfolios to find a unique "voice" for their next project. They value taste and aesthetic judgment over raw technical speed.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Technical yet poetic. Like a director's notebook found in a dark room.
- **Tone**: **Táo bạo - Sắc sảo - Sang trọng** (Bold - Sharp - Luxury).
- **Aesthetic**: "Midnight Dossier" – A high-contrast, editorial layout with film-noir influences, grain textures, and precision-engineered technical details.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark Mode (Obsidian / Deep Charcoal).
- **Accents**: Cinematic Red (#e21d1d) used for data points, markers, and focus elements.
- **Typography**: Syne (Bold Display) paired with JetBrains Mono (Technical Labels) and Inter (Premium Body).
- **Artist Touches**: Film grain overlays, custom reactive cursors, and scroll-driven parallax reveals.

### Design Principles
1. **Precision & Purpose**: Every line and marker must look like it's part of a high-end editing console.
2. **Cinematic Density**: Prefer informative, tight-knit editorial layouts over loose, generic white space.
3. **Reactive Artistry**: The UI should feel "alive" – responding to the user's presence with subtle, elegant motion.
4. **Visual Storytelling**: Use layout asymmetry to guide the eye through the "Director's Cut."
